Announcements

Life Legacy

Bible Study (Adult & Children) is held on Wednesday nights at 7 pm. Family dinner at 6:15 pm in the tower level. All are welcome. Please join us for food, fellowship and the Word.

Sunday School: Please join us for the Adult Sunday School each Sunday morning at 9am.

Looking for a church home? The next "Meet the Gees" fellowship will be Sunday, April 10 immediately following service. This fellowship allows you an opportunity to get to know Pastors Alex and Jackie and learn more about Fountain of Life. Lunch is provided.

Ministers Carlotta Calmese and Jackie Hunt will lead a five week series for Women on "The Power of Forgiveness" on Wednesday nights at 7pm starting March 30th. We will explore: What is forgiveness? What does the word say about forgiveness? Why is forgiveness so important in our healing and deliverance? How do we move toward forgiveness of self, others and even God? This class will be offered along with our regular Wednesday night Bible Study.

Our next Baptism & Baby Dedication Service will be held on Sunday, April 24th! Sign up in the Fellowship Hall.

Life Ministry

Young Adult Ministry will be hosting GAME NIGHT, This Friday March 25th at 9pm here at FOL (Lower Level). Come for music, games, fun, and fellowship. Bring a board game and a snack to share. Ages ~18-35. Please Email youngadults@folmadison.org or see Sis. Jasmine Zapata for more information. Hope to see you there!

Have you ever wondered what it's like to direct a choir? Have you ever felt music & rhythm stirring within you and wanted to teach it to others? Julia Saffold, Director of Choral Ministries, will be conducting FOL's 2nd Director's Training Class beginning Saturday, April 9, 2016 @ 3PM. Training will include the principles of directing as well as actual application. No experience necessary.

Interested in joining a Small Group? We are opening up small groups again for those who are interested in joining. We are studying "Encountering Jesus." Please see Eric Porter or Pastor Jackie to assist you with finding a group that works for you. We are also studying this book in Sunday School!
