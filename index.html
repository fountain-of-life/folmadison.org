Life Legacy

Sunday School: Please join us for the Adult Sunday School each Sunday morning at 9am.

Looking for a church home? The next "Meet the Gees" fellowship will be Sunday, March 13th immediately following service. This fellowship allows you an opportunity to get to know Pastors Alex and Jackie and learn more about Fountain of Life. Lunch is provided.

Interested in serving? The next Involvement Orientation Class will be held Sunday, March 20th immediately following the morning service. If you are interested in attending, please sign up at the back table after service or see Pastor Kevin or Bro. Eric Porter for additional information.

Life Ministry

Sing with the Women's Ensemble for Easter! All women high school aged and up are invited, and all experience levels are welcome. We'll prepare a hymn or classical-based piece for the Easter services (March 27). Rehearsals: Wednesdays, 3/2, 3/9, 3/16, 6:30-8:30pm, downstairs at FOL. RSVP to Becca Grant at bgrant@folmadison.org.

Do you play didgeridoo? Eletric guitar? The Arts Ministry is seeking skilled instrumentalists to play for the worship service in various capacities, from the occasional solo to weekly participation in the band. Contact Becca Grant to see where you could fit in! bgrant@folmadison.org.

Interested in joining a Small Group? We are opening up small groups again for those who are interested in joining. We are studying "Encountering Jesus." Please see Eric Porter or Pastor Jackie to assist you with finding a group that works for you. We are also studying this book in Sunday School!

Hospitality Ministry is seeking new members! If interested, please Sis. Margaret or sign up at the back table.

FOL College Ministry is hosting a "Resurrection Sunday Dinner" for all college students who will be staying in Madison for the holiday. Transportation is provided. RSVP IS REQUIRED! Please email Kesha to RSVP at kesha.wilkinson@impactmovement.org

FOL's Children's Ministry will be holding its annual Easter Egg Hunt on Sunday, March 20th immediately after service. Families, please join us for an afternoon of fun and fellowship! Donations of candy and plastic eggs are needed. Items may be dropped off to the Children's Ministry classrooms on Sundays or Wednesday nights during bible study between 6:30 - 8:30 PM on Wednesdays

Prayer and Healing Clinic is THIS Saturday! Once a month the FOL Prayer Ministry gather together with intercessors from around the city of Madison to ask God to heal our city and to usher His Spirit of Healing back into the earth. Join us this Saturday, March 19th from 6-8pm here at FOL.

Ministers Carlotta Calmese and Jackie Hunt will lead a five week series for Women on "The Power of Forgiveness" on Wednesday nights at 7pm starting March 30th. We will explore: What is forgiveness? What does the word say about forgiveness? Why is forgiveness so important in our healing and deliverance? How do we move toward forgiveness of self, others and even God? This class will be offered along with our regular Wednesday night Bible Study.
