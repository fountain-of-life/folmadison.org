Life Legacy

Bible Study (Adult & Children) is held on Wednesday nights at 7 pm. Family dinner at 6:15 pm in the tower level. All are welcome. Please join us for food, fellowship and the Word.

Sunday School: Please join us for the Adult Sunday School each Sunday morning at 9am.

Looking for a church home? The next "Meet the Gees" fellowship will be Sunday, April 10 immediately following service. This fellowship allows you an opportunity to get to know Pastors Alex and Jackie and learn more about Fountain of Life. Lunch is provided.

Interested in serving? The next Involvement Orientation Class will be held Sunday, April 17th immediately following the morning service. If you are interested in attending, please sign up at the back table after service or see Pastor Kevin or Bro. Eric Porter for additional information.

Life Ministry

Do you play didgeridoo? Theremin? Electric guitar? The Arts Ministry is seeking skilled instrumentalists to play for the worship service in various capacities, from the occassional solo to weekly participation in the band. Contact Becca Grant to see where you could fit in! bgrant@folmadison.org

Interested in joining a Small Group? We are opening up small groups again for those who are interested in joining. We are studying "Encountering Jesus." Please see Eric Porter or Pastor Jackie to assist you with finding a group that works for you. We are also studying this book in Sunday School!

Hospitality Ministry is seeking new members! If interested, please Sis. Margaret or sign up at the back table.

College Sunday is back!!! On April 24th college students will be leading the service worshipping God through song, spoken word, dance and more. You won't want to miss it!

Ministers Carlotta Calmese and Jackie Hunt will lead a five week series for Women on "The Power of Forgiveness" on Wednesday nights at 7pm starting March 30th. We will explore: What is forgiveness? What does the word say about forgiveness? Why is forgiveness so important in our healing and deliverance? How do we move toward forgiveness of self, others and even God? This class will be offered along with our regular Wednesday night Bible Study.

FOL Creatieve Collection Calling all artists and creatives! Did you know that FOL has a space for you? FOL Creative Collective is a team that is at a place where we want to see where God can take our community of artists. So join us the evening of April 6th from 6:30-8:30pm at church to learn more about our vision and share your ideas! Food and refreshments will be provided.

Have you ever wondered what it's like to direct a choir? Have you ever felt music & rhythm stirring within you and wanted to teach it to others? Training will include the principles of directing as well as actual application. No experience necessary.
